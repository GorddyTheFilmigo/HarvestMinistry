About Us

Who We Are & What We Believe

Our Story

The Lords Harvest Ministry is a Christ-centered mission founded with a burning passion to reach the lost, share the gospel, and build communities grounded in the Word of God. Inspired by Acts 13:47, we embrace the mandate to be a light to the nations.

Our Vision

To see individuals, families, and nations transformed through the power of the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To reach the lost, disciple believers, and empower communities through teaching, outreach, worship, and compassionate service.

Our Core Beliefs

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God
  • Salvation comes through Jesus Christ alone
  • We are called to evangelize and disciple
  • We value prayer, worship, community, and service
